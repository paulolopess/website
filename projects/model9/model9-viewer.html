KRYSSET CHAIR

KRYSSET
LOUNGE CHAIR

FREDRIK A. KAYSER

NORWAY

Designed in 1955, is a timeless Norwegian masterpiece.
Its name, "Krysset", meaning the cross, reflects its distinctive cross-legged frame.
Known for its elegant craftsmanship, the chair features a stretched leather backrest and premium materials like oak and walnut, combining style and comfort in a mid-century design icon.
